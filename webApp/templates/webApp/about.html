The COVID-19 is an unparalleled crisis leading to a huge number of casualties and security problems. To reduce the spread of coronavirus, people often wear masks to protect themselves. This makes face recognition a very difficult task since certain parts of the face are hidden. We've thus built an application that render users' camera and captures each instance to check the whether user has worn a mask or not.
